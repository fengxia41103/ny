About us

PY Consulting focuses on building proof-of-concept web application for small to mid-size companies using Django framework and other related web technologies. A common cycle spans between 4 to 10 weeks carrying product from raw concept to a deployed prototype. This helps client to visualize his idea and to promote constructive discussion with his R&D team or outsourced company. Further, the prototype helps to clarify and define key components within the final system architecture, therefore assisting clients in estimating future system's capability and scalability.
